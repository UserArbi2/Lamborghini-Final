The Gallardo Experience

The Lamborghini Gallardo was the brand's most successful model to date, representing a pivotal moment in Lamborghini's history. As the first model developed under Audi ownership, it combined Italian passion with German engineering precision to create a supercar that redefined the category.

With its aggressive styling, iconic V10 engine note, and performance that rivaled cars twice its price, the Gallardo brought Lamborghini to a wider audience while retaining the brand's exotic appeal. It laid the groundwork for the modern Lamborghini lineup and stands as one of the most important vehicles in the company's storied history.

Key Features

Iconic V10 Engine

The heart of the Gallardo is its naturally aspirated V10 engine, delivering exhilarating performance with a distinctive, spine-tingling soundtrack that's instantly recognizable.

E-Gear Transmission

Revolutionary for its time, the paddle-shift e-gear transmission delivers lightning-fast shifts and multiple driving modes for both track performance and daily usability.

All-Wheel Drive

The Gallardo's sophisticated all-wheel drive system provides incredible traction and stability in all conditions while maintaining the dynamic handling expected of a Lamborghini.

Aluminum Construction

The all-aluminum space frame chassis and body panels keep weight to a minimum while maintaining exceptional structural rigidity for precise handling.
