The First HPEV Lamborghini Supercar

Fusion of electric power and V12 performance

The Revuelto Experience

The Lamborghini Revuelto represents a new era for Lamborghini, harmoniously combining the raw power of a naturally aspirated V12 engine with cutting-edge hybrid technology. As the successor to the legendary Aventador, the Revuelto takes Lamborghini's flagship supercar into the electrified future without compromising the visceral driving experience that defines the brand.

Every aspect of the Revuelto has been engineered to deliver unprecedented performance while embracing sustainable technology. From its revolutionary High Performance Electrified Vehicle (HPEV) architecture to its aerospace-inspired design, the Revuelto continues Lamborghini's tradition of pushing boundaries while setting new standards for the supercar of tomorrow.
